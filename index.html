About Us

Welcome to LifestyeShi, a premier photography studio founded in 2023 by Ibiso Don-pedro, a committed and ambitious photographer based in Mararaba, Nassarawa State. Our studio is dedicated to capturing life's beautiful moments with creativity and passion.

At LifestyeShi, we believe that every photograph tells a unique story, and our mission is to immortalize those stories through our lens. Whether it's a portrait session, a wedding, or a special event, we strive to deliver exceptional quality and customer satisfaction.
